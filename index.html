ConsultApp

A Client Management Site for Consultants on the Go

ConsultApp

ConsultApp has a visitors view with Projects, an about page, and a contact section, as well as an admin section where consultants can manage their expenses and notes

Projects

A simple view for potential customers to see your work and what you've done

Client List

In the admin view, the first thing you'll see is a list of your clients

Selected Client

Select any of your clients, and add notes and hours worked

Add New Client

Or add a new client on the fly.

ConsultApp was written in Angular 2 with Bootstrap styling heavily emphasized throughout the website. The idea was conceived as a way to help my dad organize his clients and more effectively manage his time and expenses.

  • Client: Maxfield Group, LLC.
  • Category: Full Stack Web Development

Stock-X

Historical stock data. See what moves your stocks.

Stock-X was created to fill a gap that I had found while researching some stocks. The goal of the procet was to connect the highs and lows of a companies stock price with headlines about the company from that day.

Stock-X was built with Angular 1.5 components, sass, gulp, as well as the New York Times APi and the Yahoo Finance API
